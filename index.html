Have Any Questions?

1. Can I Trust the Accuracy of the Sizes Listed Above?
Yes, you can trust the accuracy of the sizes listed on our website. We strive to ensure that all size measurements are as precise as possible. However, please note that minor variations can occur due to the nature of product manufacturing. We recommend referring to our size guide for detailed information, which is designed to help you make the best choice for your needs.
2. How to Accurately Measure Your Ring Size at Home?
Measuring your ring size at home is straightforward. You can use a strip of paper or a piece of string. Wrap it around the base of your finger, mark where the ends meet, and then measure the length against a ruler. This measurement corresponds to the circumference of your finger. Compare this measurement with our ring size chart to find your perfect size. For the most accurate results, measure your finger at the end of the day when it is likely to be at its largest.
3. Is It Possible to Place an Order Using My Local Size Measurements?
Absolutely! We understand the importance of getting the perfect fit. You can place an order using your local size measurements. Our size conversion chart is available to help you translate your local size into our standard sizing. If you have any doubts or need assistance, our customer service team is here to help you select the right size.
4. What Are the Terms of Your Ring Re-Sizing Policy?
Our re-sizing policy is designed to ensure your complete satisfaction. If your ring doesn't fit perfectly, we offer a one-time free resizing within the first 30 days of purchase. The ring can be resized within one full size up or down from the original size. Please note that some designs might not be resizable due to their unique characteristics. For such cases, we will offer alternative solutions. Contact our customer service team for assistance and to understand the specific terms related to your purchase.
5. How Can I Get Assistance in Finding My Correct Ring Size?

Absolutely, our dedicated team is ready to assist you in selecting the right size. For any help, please feel free to email us at hello@diamondrensu.com, or you can also schedule a virtual appointment with us for more detailed assistance.
